Cross-platform compatibility


Cross-platform compatibility is a key feature of top game engines. It allows developers to create games that can be played on a wide range of devices, from PCs to consoles to mobile phones.   • Benefits of cross-platform compatibility

    • Increased sales potential: By making their games available on multiple platforms, developers can reach a wider audience and increase the potential sales of their games.
