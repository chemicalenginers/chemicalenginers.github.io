To set Google as the default search engine in Chrome, users can follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the Chrome browser.
  2. Click on the three vertical dots in the top-right corner of the browser window.
  3. Select "Settings."
  4. Scroll down to the "Search engine" section.
  5. Click on the drop-down menu and select "Google."
  6. Click on the "Set as default" button.
