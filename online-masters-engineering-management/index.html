Question 2: Can an OMasterEM be completed while working full-time?


Yes, the flexibility of online learning makes it possible to complete an OMasterEM while maintaining a full-time job. Programs are designed to accommodate the busy schedules of working professionals, with flexible deadlines and asynchronous course delivery.
