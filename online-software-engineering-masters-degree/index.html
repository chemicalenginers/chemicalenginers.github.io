The cost of an online software engineering master's degree varies depending on the program. However, most programs cost between $20,000 and $40,000.

Question 6: How long does it take to complete an online software engineering master's degree?

The time it takes to complete an online software engineering master's degree varies depending on the program. However, most programs can be completed in two to three years.
