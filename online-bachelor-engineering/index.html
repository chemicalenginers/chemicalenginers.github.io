Question 3: Are online bachelor's in engineering programs accredited?

Answer: Yes, many online bachelor's in engineering programs are accredited by the Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology (ABET). Graduates of ABET-accredited engineering programs are eligible to sit for the Fundamentals of Engineering (FE) exam, which is the first step towards becoming a licensed professional engineer.
