Making Google your search engine involves setting it as the default search provider in your web browser. This means that when you type a search query into the browser's address bar or search box, Google will be used to perform the search and display the results.

There are several benefits to making Google your search engine. Google is one of the most popular and comprehensive search engines in the world, with a vast index of websites and other online content. It also offers a variety of features to help you refine your searches, such as filters, operators, and advanced search options.

To make Google your search engine, open your web browser and go to the Settings or Preferences menu. Under the Search section, you should find an option to set the default search engine. Select Google from the list of options and click Save or OK to confirm your changes.
